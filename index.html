I know you're busy, so I want to make this as valuable and efficient as possible. We'll be covering your current coverage, identifying any gaps, and discussing how your agency can help protect everything you've worked so hard to build.

üè† Family Protection & Long-Term Goals Transition

"Before we dive into the numbers, I'd love to understand your situation better. When we think about insurance protection, we're really talking about two main things:

First, protecting your family's lifestyle - making sure that if something were to happen to you, they could maintain their current standard of living.

Second, protecting your paycheck - ensuring that your income stream is secure so you can continue building toward your long-term goals like retirement, your children's education, or that dream home.

"Tell me, what are your main concerns when it comes to protecting your family's future?"

‚öñÔ∏è How Liability Works - The Lawyer Example

"Let me give you a real example of how liability protection works. Imagine you're driving and you accidentally hit a successful lawyer who makes $300,000 a year.

Potential Lawsuit Breakdown:
  • Medical Bills: $200,000
  • Lost Wages: $600,000 (2 years at $300k/year)
  • Pain & Suffering: $400,000
  • Legal Fees: $100,000
  • Total Potential Lawsuit: $1,300,000

"This is exactly why adequate liability protection is so crucial. Your current auto policy limits might only be $250,000, which means you're personally responsible for over $1 million. That's why we need to make sure your coverage matches your actual exposure."
